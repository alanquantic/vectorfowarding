Manufacturing in Mexico

Assembling products in Mexico has become a strategic advantage.

During the last decade, assembly in Mexico has become increasingly popular due to rising shipping costs from Asia and increases in U.S. wages.

Over the past several decades, U.S. and other foreign companies have relied on Mexico for their manufacturing needs, benefiting from lower labor costs, closer proximity, and strong quality standards.

Contract Manufacturing

Ramp up production with lower capital commitment.

Contract manufacturing is best suited for early stage and growth stage companies that have demand for a developed product but do not yet have the infrastructure to satisfy increasing order flow.

This model allows companies to increase production and reduce costs with a much lower capital commitment than building a full scale manufacturing facility in Mexico from scratch.
